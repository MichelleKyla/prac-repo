Family

Since I am an only child, my family consists of three—my mom, my dad, and me. We're a small but close-knit group, and though it's just the three of us, we've built a strong bond over the years. Growing up as the only child has shaped a lot of my experiences, from being independent to having a deep connection with my parents. Even though we rarely take pictures of our moments, most of which are forgotten, I think what happens off camera is more precious for me. I am forever grateful for them.
